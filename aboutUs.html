About Us

Who We Are

We are passionate about supporting tribal artisans by providing them with a platform to showcase and sell their handcrafted items. Our mission is to connect these talented artisans with customers and businesses around the world, promoting the preservation and appreciation of their traditional crafts.

Our Solutions

  • Global platform for showcasing handcrafted items
  • Marketplace for both local and international buyers
  • Tools for artisans to manage product listings and orders
  • Secure transaction and payment processing
  • Cultural consultation for accurate representation
  • Promotions and discounts to attract customers

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to supporting artisans and promoting cultural heritage. Here are some of the key roles within the platform:

  • Sravya - Manages platform content, monitors transactions, and resolves issues.
  • Akhil - Creates and updates product listings, manages orders, and communicates with customers.
  • Pravalika - Explores and purchases handcrafted items, provides reviews, and participates in promotions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower tribal artisans by providing them with a global platform that preserves their cultural heritage and offers sustainable livelihoods. We strive to foster a deeper connection between these artisans and a global audience, creating a marketplace that values tradition and craftsmanship.
